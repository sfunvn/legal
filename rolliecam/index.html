Support

We're here to help. RollieCam turns your iPhone into a pocket film camera — analog looks previewed live, right in the viewfinder. If something isn't working or you have a question or idea, reach out — we read every message.

Email support

How do I send feedback or report a bug?

Email us at support@silverai.com, or tap Settings → Send feedback in the app. Telling us your device model and iOS version helps us help you faster.

How do the live film looks work?

Swipe through the roll of looks at the bottom of the viewfinder and the scene transforms in real time before you shoot. RollieCam is WYSIWYG — the look you frame is the look that's baked into the photo when you press the shutter, so there's no heavy editing afterward.

What are RAW and Log capture for?

RAW and Log keep the most image data for maximum quality and editing flexibility — useful if you want to grade a shot further later. They produce larger files than a standard capture, so enable them in Pro controls only when you need that latitude.

Where are my photos stored?

Shots are saved to RollieCam's built-in gallery on your device — not your system Photos and not any cloud. They appear in your iOS Photo Library only if you turn on “Also save to Photos” or export a shot yourself from the gallery.

How do I use the grid, level, and framing tools?

Toggle the grid overlay and level indicator from the viewfinder controls to nail your composition. They're guides only — they're never baked into the photo and don't affect the film look you capture.

Does the app upload or track my data?

No. RollieCam makes no network calls and contains no analytics, tracking, ads, or third-party SDKs. Your photos never leave your device. See the Privacy Policy for details.
