Privacy Policy

Effective 17 June 2026

Task Cam is built to keep your work private. Everything you record stays on your device. We run no servers for this app — your photos, videos, and their metadata are never uploaded to us or anyone else.

What we store No data collection Permissions Your control Children Contact

1 Who we are

Task Cam (“the app”) is developed by Silver AI (“we”, “us”). You can reach us any time at support@silverai.com.

2 What the app stores, and where

Task Cam is a dedicated, on-device camera roll for work. The photos and videos you capture, the folders you organize them into, your settings, and any location or timestamp attached to a capture are all stored locally on your device, inside the app’s own storage. This data:

  • is never transmitted off your device by the app;
  • is not shared with us or any third party;
  • is removed when you delete the item from Trash, or when you uninstall the app.

3 We do not collect data

The app contains no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no third-party SDKs, and it makes no network calls. We do not create user accounts, and we have no access to your content.

4 Device permissions we request

The app asks for the following permissions only to provide its features. You can grant or revoke each one in iOS Settings at any time.

  • Camera — to capture the photos and videos you take in the app.
  • Microphone — to record sound while you are filming a video.
  • Location (While Using the App) — only if you turn on “Save location”: to geotag a capture with where it was taken. This is optional and can be turned off in settings; with it off, no location is attached.
  • Photo Library (Add only) — only if you choose “Also save to Photos” or export an item: to copy that photo or video into your system Photo Library. The app does not read your existing Photos.

5 Your control over your data

  • Delete individual items (Trash), or empty the Trash to remove them permanently.
  • Set per-folder auto-delete rules.
  • Export items to the Files app or share them, on your initiative.
  • Uninstall the app to remove all of its locally stored data.

6 Children

Task Cam is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

7 Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the effective date above and post the new version at this URL.

8 Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@silverai.com.
